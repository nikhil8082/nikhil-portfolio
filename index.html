AI-Based Digital Stethoscope

Developed a portable health monitoring device using Raspberry Pi Pico W with MAX9814 microphone amplifier, MAX30102 pulse oximeter, and DS18B20 temperature sensor. Features SSD1306 OLED display for real-time vital signs. Powered by 18650 Li-ion battery with TP4056 charging module — an affordable all-in-one monitor for healthcare settings.

Healthcare IoT Sensors

Smart Iron Box

Retrofitted a household iron with Arduino Nano and 4 MG90S servo motors (2 on each side near heating plate) for motion-based idle-detection. When stationary, servos automatically lift the iron off the surface — a fully switch-free safety mechanism preventing burns and fire hazards.
